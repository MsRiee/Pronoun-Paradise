🌴 Pronoun Island Quick Reference

👤 Personal Pronouns

Replace names of people or things.
Subject: I, you, he, she, it, we, they.
Object: me, him, her, us, them.

🔑 Possessive Pronouns

Show ownership. They stand alone!
Examples: mine, yours, his, hers, its, ours, theirs.

💥 Intensive Pronouns

Add special emphasis back to a noun. Can be removed completely!
Examples: myself, yourself, himself, herself, itself, ourselves, themselves.

👉 Demonstrative Pronouns

Point out specific things.
Examples: this (near/singular), that (far/singular), these (near/plural), those (far/plural).

❓ Indefinite Pronouns

Do not refer to any specific person or thing.
Examples: everyone, someone, nobody, everything, any, both, few, many, each, all.
